Explanation

This Profit Calculator helps you measure profitability by comparing cost price (CP) and selling price (SP). It shows profit or loss, plus two commonly-used percentages:

  • Profit % (on cost): profit relative to your cost
  • Margin % (on selling): profit relative to your selling price
  • Markup % (on cost): same base as profit%, often used in pricing

Tip: If you are running ads/shipping/packaging, add those costs into CP to get a realistic net profit.

FAQs

Markup vs margin?

Markup is calculated on cost (CP). Margin is calculated on selling price (SP).

Include overheads?

Yes for net profit: include shipping, packaging, ads, rent, salaries, etc. in cost.

Negative profit?

It means a loss (SP is lower than CP).

Taxes?

Profit after tax can differ. This calculator shows pre-tax profit unless tax is included in costs.

Units?

Use the same currency for CP and SP. The calculator only formats output.
