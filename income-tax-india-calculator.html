Explanation

This Income Tax Calculator (India) gives a planning estimate using a slab-based approach. You can compare New Regime vs Old Regime, apply a standard deduction, and add common deductions like 80C and 80D.

Tip: If your deductions are low, New regime often looks better. If you have strong eligible deductions/exemptions, Old regime may reduce tax. Always verify with official rules or a CA for filing.

FAQs

Which financial year should I choose?

Select the year you want to estimate for. Rules can change between years. If unsure, use ‚ÄúCustom‚Äù and update slab JSON.

Is TDS same as tax payable?

No. TDS is tax deducted during the year. Final tax payable/refund depends on your total income, deductions, and actual liability.

Does this include cess?

Yes, this includes 4% Health & Education Cess on computed tax (and surcharge if applied).

New vs Old regime‚Äîwhat should I choose?

New regime often has lower slab rates but fewer deductions. Old regime can be beneficial if you have significant eligible deductions/exemptions.

Should I consult a CA?

Yes for complex cases (multiple income sources, capital gains, business income, exemptions, foreign income, etc.). This tool is for quick estimation and planning.
