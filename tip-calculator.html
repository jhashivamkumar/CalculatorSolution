Explanation

This Tip Calculator helps you quickly estimate the tip amount based on a percentage and split the final total among multiple people. You can optionally add tax/service charge and choose whether the tip is calculated on the bill only or on bill + tax. Rounding options are included to make payments easier.

Tip: If a service charge is already included on your receipt, you may choose a smaller extra tip or skip it entirely.
