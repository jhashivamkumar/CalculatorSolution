    Explanation

    Credit scores are generally influenced by factors like payment history, credit utilization, length of credit history, new credit inquiries, and credit mix. This calculator combines your inputs into an educational estimate so you can understand which areas may be pulling your score up or down.

    Formula (simple educational weighting)

    Estimate = Base + PaymentHistory + Utilization + CreditAge + Inquiries + Mix

Payment history: higher on-time % adds points
Utilization: lower % adds points (best typically under ~30%)
Age: older history adds points
Inquiries: more inquiries subtract points
Mix: healthy mix adds points
