FAQs

VAT vs GST?

Different names/structures by country but similar concept.

Reverse charge?

Applies in certain B2B transactions; reporting differs by country.

Inclusive vs exclusive?

Inclusive prices already include VAT; exclusive prices need VAT added on top.

Rounding?

Use rounding to match invoice rules (often 2 decimals).

Thresholds?

Some businesses may be below VAT registration thresholds depending on the country.
