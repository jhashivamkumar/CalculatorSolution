FAQs

Which index?

Use CPI or another official price index for your country/category when possible.

Estimate future inflation?

You can assume a rate, but real-world inflation can change. Use conservative estimates.

Nominal vs real?

Nominal is raw money. Real value adjusts for inflation to reflect purchasing power.

Compound?

Yes, inflation compounds over time, so even small rates matter in long horizons.

Use for salary planning?

Yes. It helps estimate how much salary growth is needed to maintain buying power.
