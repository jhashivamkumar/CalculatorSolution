    Explanation

    Crypto ROI (return on investment) tells you the percentage gain or loss from a trade. The most common formula is (Sell ‚àí Buy) √∑ Buy √ó 100. In real trading, your net results depend on exchange trading fees, and sometimes network/gas fees. This calculator computes: gross ROI, net ROI after fees, and a break-even sell price (the sell price needed to cover fees).
