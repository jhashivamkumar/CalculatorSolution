Explanation

This Subscription Pricing Calculator helps you estimate recurring revenue based on your plan tiers and customers. It calculates MRR (monthly recurring revenue), ARR (annual run-rate), ARPU, and the impact of churn on growth. You can also estimate LTV (lifetime value) and CAC payback using a simple, commonly-used approximation.

Tip: Small churn improvements can dramatically increase LTV. If you are testing price changes, compare MRR and churn sensitivity side-by-side.
