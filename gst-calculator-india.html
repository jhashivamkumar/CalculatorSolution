FAQs

How many GST slabs?

Common slabs: 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%.

Is GST included in MRP?

Often yes, but always verify on the invoice/label.

Reverse charge?

Reverse charge applies in specific cases and depends on the transaction type.

How to compute exclusive price?

Exclusive = Inclusive / (1 + rate/100). GST = Inclusive ‚àí Exclusive.

Rounding rules?

Follow invoice rounding used by the seller; commonly 2 decimals.
