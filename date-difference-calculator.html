Explanation

The Date Difference Calculator determines the time span between two calendar dates. It converts the difference into multiple formats such as total days, weeks, months, years, and even hours or minutes for precise planning.

This tool is useful for tracking deadlines, calculating age, measuring project durations, planning subscriptions, and comparing timelines accurately.

Formula

Difference = End Date − Start Date
