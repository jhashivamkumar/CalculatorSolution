Explanation

This Water Intake Calculator estimates your daily water target using your weight as the base, then adjusts the recommendation based on activity level and climate/heat. The result is shown in liters, cups, and bottles per day to make it easy to follow.

Reminder: thirst, urine color, and how you feel matter. If you have kidney/heart conditions, are pregnant, or have been advised to restrict fluids, follow medical guidance.

Formula

Base water (L/day) = weight_kg √ó 0.035
Adjusted (L/day) = (Base + activity_add + climate_add) √ó goal_multiplier
Cups (250 ml each) = liters / 0.25
Bottles/day = liters / bottle_size_liters
