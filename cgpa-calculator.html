Explanation

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the weighted average of your semester GPAs, where the weight is the number of credits in each semester. This calculator works for both 10-point and 4-point systems (just keep everything on the same scale).

FAQs

Transfer credits?

Follow your institution rules. Some transfer credits may be excluded from CGPA calculation.

Different scales?

Use one consistent scale. If you mix scales (4.0 and 10.0), normalize first using your institution method.

Exemptions?

Some subjects may be pass/fail and not counted in GPA. Enter only what your institute includes.

Rounding?

This tool shows a clean rounded CGPA; use your institute‚Äôs rounding rule for official results.

Appeals?

Grade appeals are handled by your institution. Recalculate after changes are updated.
