Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple measure to assess body weight relative to height. This guide explains how to calculate BMI, interpret categories, and understand limitations.

Calculating BMI

BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m))². Use our BMI Calculator to compute your value quickly for metric or imperial units.

Interpretation

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: 25–29.9
  • Obesity: ≥ 30

Limitations

BMI does not measure body fat directly and may misclassify muscular individuals. Consider additional measures like waist circumference and body composition when assessing health.

Next steps

If concerned about your BMI or weight, consult a healthcare professional for tailored guidance.
